About me

Hi! My name is Alexa Malave,
Aya.x to friends and clients :)

I’m a graphic designer who enjoys illustration and am very interested in developing as a picture book illustrator.

I’ve done some stationary work, web design, animation and practiced some character design.

I tend to play some with my own illustration style to the point of being adaptable to different moods or ages as needed. I believe the same can be said about myself, I find it easy to accommodate other’s preferences within personal boundaries and beliefs

I am presently awaiting graduation and hoping to apply the knowledge received and the experience acquired from academic and volunteering projects.
